Summoning Ritual of the Silver Flame

Casting Time: 90 Minutes

The Summoning Ritual of the Silver Flame is a rite practiced to invite a guardian presence of light and protection. This ritual blends flame, metal, water, and scent into a single act of intention. Each element serves a purpose: silver for clarity, salt for boundaries, oil for transformation, and flame for manifestation. When performed with patience and focus, the Silver Flame is said to answer, rising gently in the stillness to strengthen and protect the summoner.

Ingredients

  • 3 sprigs of dried sage
  • 1 silver coin, polished clean
  • 1 vial of spring water
  • 5 drops of frankincense oil
  • A small bowl for mixing
  • A circle of salt (enough to encircle your ritual space)
  • 1 white candle
  • A personal token (such as a ring, feather, or keepsake)

Instructions

  1. Cleanse the space by burning the sage and letting its smoke drift around the area.
  2. Lay a circle of salt around your working space, leaving enough room to sit within it.
  3. Place the silver coin in the center of the circle and set the white candle beside it.
  4. Light the candle and focus on the steady flame as a symbol of clarity and intention.
  5. Place your personal token near the flame to connect the ritual to yourself.
  6. In the small bowl, pour the vial of spring water and add five drops of frankincense oil. Stir gently three times clockwise.
  7. Sprinkle a few drops of the scented water around the salt circle, sealing the boundary.
  8. Place one drop of the scented water directly on the silver coin, watching how the flame reflects off the wet surface
  9. Sit quietly, gazing into the candle, and imagine a silver flame rising and surrounding you in light.
  10. When the flame flickers or your intuition tells you the ritual is complete, thank the presence.
  11. Extinguish the candle and carefully dismantle the circle of salt, scattering it outside to release the energy.
