Conjuration of the Shadow and Bloom

Casting Time: 120 Minutes

The Conjuration of the Shadow and Bloom is a ritual designed to summon a spirit companion. Its form is not fixed — the summoned being reflects the intent of the summoner. When performed with a heart leaning toward mischief, cunning, or hidden power, the rite calls forth an imp-like familiar. When performed with gentleness, curiosity, or reverence, the same working instead calls a fey-like companion. The materials of the ritual are neutral; the guiding hand is the will and intent of the one who performs it.

Ingredients

  • A circle of salt, wide enough to sit within
  • 7 dried rose petals
  • 1 feather (preferably found, not bought)
  • 1 shard of obsidian or dark stone
  • 1 small bell or chime
  • 3 drops of honey
  • 1 black candle and 1 green candle
  • A shallow bowl of fresh water

Instructions

  1. Mark a circle with salt, ensuring you will not be disturbed inside it.
  2. Place the black candle on the left side of the circle and the green candle on the right.
  3. Position the bowl of fresh water at the center, placing the obsidian shard on its rim.
  4. Scatter the rose petals evenly around the circle, symbolizing the threshold between worlds.
  5. Hold the feather above the water and drip three drops of honey onto its tip. Let the honey dissolve into the water as you release the feather into the bowl.
  6. Light both candles, one after the other. As you do, state your purpose aloud — what kind of companion you wish to call.
  7. If your intent is sharp, cunning, or mischievous, fix your gaze on the black candle. The shadows it casts will guide the imp.
  8. If your intent is gentle, curious, or protective, fix your gaze on the green candle. Its glow will guide the fey..
  9. Sprinkle a few drops of the scented water around the salt circle, sealing the boundary.
  10. Place one drop of the scented water directly on the silver coin, watching how the flame reflects off the wet surface
  11. Sit quietly, gazing into the candle, and imagine a silver flame rising and surrounding you in light.
  12. When the flame flickers or your intuition tells you the ritual is complete, thank the presence.
  13. Extinguish the candle and carefully dismantle the circle of salt, scattering it outside to release the energy.
