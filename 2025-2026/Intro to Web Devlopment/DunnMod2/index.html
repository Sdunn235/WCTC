Take a look at My Gremoire!

✨If magic had a kitchen, this would be my cookbook. Here you’ll find bubbling brews, sparkly spells, and the occasional singed corner from experiments that got a little too enthusiastic. Think of it as a magical pantry — stocked with healing charms, fire tricks, and “just in case” enchantments for when life throws a goblin at you. I’ll share my favorites the way any good spell-slinger would: with a mix of practical notes, a dash of personal flair, and plenty of stardust on the margins. So, pour yourself a cup of moon tea, settle in, and let’s cook up some magic together. ✨

Summoning Ritual of the Silver Flame

Casting Time: 90 Minutes

A timeless ritual of light and protection, the Silver Flame is called through coin, salt, and candlefire. Its glow wards off shadows and steadies the spirit. Gentle yet unyielding, the flame becomes both shield and guide. To glimpse its radiance is to feel safe in the dark.

Conjuration of the Shadow and Bloom

Casting Time: 120 Minutes

A ritual of dual nature, this summoning bends to the caster’s heart. Mischief and cunning call forth an imp, while curiosity and care awaken a fey. Both bloom from the same circle, shaped only by intent. The outcome lies in what you dare to invite.

The Transmutaion Ritual of the Living Wands

Casting Time: 150 Minutes

Crafting a wand is more than joining wood and crystal—it is binding self to tool. This rite channels your essence into a branch chosen by fate and hand. As the flame warms it and the earth steadies it, the wand awakens. When complete, it is no mere stick but an extension of you.

A Classic Envocation Spell: Fireball!

Casting Time: 6-10 Seconds

Few spells are as iconic as the fireball: raw flame shaped into a sphere of will. Chili, ember, and candle spark its birth. Control is the key, for a fireball answers recklessness with chaos. To master it is to hold the storm of fire in your palm.
