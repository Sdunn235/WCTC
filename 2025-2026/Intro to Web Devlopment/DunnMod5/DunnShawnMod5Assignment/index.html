About THE DRAGONFLY HARPERS

The Dragonfly Harpers are a guild of handlers and beasts united by instinct, tradition, and spectacle. Formed beneath the canopy of Great Mother’s Roots, their hunts are streamed to millions—part ritual, part competition. Each pairing tells a story of balance: courage against chaos, handler against nature’s will. From the volcanic nests of Glavenus to the frozen hollows of Garuga, their matches test not just strength but trust between living weapons and the ones who guide them.

Bound by an oath of empathy and control, the Harpers train alongside their monsters for years before entering the arena. Their goal is not domination but harmony—to draw out the full potential of both soul and scale. Every victory honors the old songs of the hunt, every loss teaches restraint. To join their ranks is to understand that taming a monster begins within.

Upcoming Events

All Hollows Eve

As twilight falls over the arenas, the veil between realms thins. Handlers face beasts born from shadow and memory, testing bonds in the dim firelight. It’s less a tournament than a reckoning—where courage is weighed, and even the air trembles with the echoes of forgotten hunts.

Winter Solstice Match

When the frost claims the battleground, only the bold remain. Handlers and monsters alike fight against time and temperature, their breath steaming in the frozen dusk. Victory here isn’t measured by power alone, but by endurance, trust, and the will to burn bright against the dark.

Spring Equinox

As the world thaws, the Harpers gather to celebrate renewal in combat. Fresh trainees pair with young beasts in symbolic duels that mark the year’s first awakening. The roars echo through blooming fields, reminding all that every season begins with a challenge—and a chance to rise again.
