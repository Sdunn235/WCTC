Gemma Stark

Glavenus

Gemma Stark was born in the forge cities that glow red even at midnight, where every child learns the cadence of hammer and heat before they learn to write. A blacksmith’s apprentice turned combat engineer, she built her first weapon to control the fury she saw in others—and eventually, in herself. Her encounter with Glavenus was no accident; she sought the beast after witnessing it melt an entire caravan’s armor line in a single sweep. Their battle lasted six hours, the ash thick enough to blot out the sun. When it ended, both lay collapsed amid the slag, the air shimmering from exhaustion and acceptance. Since then, Gemma and Glavenus have moved as one entity—she the tactician, it the embodiment of elemental violence. Their fighting style blends metallurgy and motion: preheating the arena sands to warp enemy footing, redirecting molten debris into calculated arcs, and using each clash to fuel the Glavenus’s blade-tail to greater fury. Off the field, Gemma runs the Harpers’ forgehouse, maintaining weapons for other handlers while studying how emotional focus can alter a beast’s elemental resonance. To her, every battle is a forging—each strike a chance to temper something wild into something enduring. Her creed, engraved above her forge, reads simply: “Only what survives the fire deserves the name.”
